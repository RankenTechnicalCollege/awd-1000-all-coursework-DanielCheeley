About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescues is a member of the Wildlife Rehabilitators Association

We help many animals, such as:

  • Raccoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles

Did you find an injured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
