Cinnamon Roll & Honeybun Cheesecake

Crust:
  • 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbles
  • 1/4 cup of sugar granulated
  • 1/2 cup butter that is melted
Cinnamon Roll Filling:
  • Half cup brown sugar
  • 2 TBSP ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter that is unsalted, melting
Cheesecake Filling:
  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup of sugar granulated
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Steps to make:

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    The oven should be preheated until 325degF (163degC). Within a large bowl mix the crumbs of graham crackers, sugar granulated and melted butter until they are well-combined. Make sure the mixture is evenly distributed across the bottom of the springform pan that is 9 inches in diameter. Put aside while you prepare the fillings.

  2. Make the Cinnamon Roll Filling:

    Within a bowl combine brown sugar with the ground cinnamon. Incorporate the butter until it melts. Stir till the mix is dry. Reserve the space for the cheesecake layering process.

  3. Prepare the Cheesecake Filling:

    In a large bowl combine the softened cream cheese as well as sugar granulated till smooth smooth. Mix in Vanilla extract and mix thoroughly. Incorporate eggs one at a time. Add the eggs, one at one at a time and beat thoroughly every time you add eggs. Mix with thick cream until completely blended.

  4. Layer the Cheesecake & Swirl the cinnamon Filling:

    Sprinkle part of the cake batter over the crust you have prepared. Pour one-third of the filling from cinnamon rolls by dollops on top of the cheesecake. Make use of a knife or toothpick to create swirls your cinnamon mixture into the batter. Sprinkle top with the remainder of cheesecake over the the top. Continue this stirring method by adding the rest of the cinnamon mixture

  5. Bake the Cheesecake:

    Put the dessert in oven, and bake for 60 to 70 hours and until the centre has set while the sides are slightly golden. The center could still move little. Shut the oven off and allow the cheesecake to sit in the oven to cool, keeping the door open for about an hour. Remove the baking dish from the oven and allow to cool for at minimum four hours, or even overnight.

  6. Prepare the Honeybun Topping:

    Within a bowl mix together powdered sugar as well as milk along with vanilla extract until it is smooth. Pour glaze glaze over the cheesecake prior to serving.
